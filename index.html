The Process

Simple from enquiry to delivery.

You tell us what you need. We handle everything else — sourcing, negotiation, quality check, documentation, and shipping.

01

Submit your requirement

Use the quote form below. Tell us the product, quantity, and your destination port. We respond within 24 hours.

02

We source and quote

We identify verified Indian suppliers, negotiate the best price, and send you a clear, itemized quote with our 5% service fee included.

03

Confirm and pay 30%

Approve the quote and pay 30% advance. We begin procurement and keep you updated at every step until goods are ready.

04

Balance at loading

Before we ship, you pay the remaining 70%. We handle customs documentation, freight, and hand you the shipping documents.
